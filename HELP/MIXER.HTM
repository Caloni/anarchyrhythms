Mixer Controls

The mixer controls the main parameters for each of the four sound channels.

Whichever sound you click on, becomes the 'selected channel'. This is the channel whose properties are displayed in the Edit Pane

Here's what the mixer parameters do:

Length pot
Feedback level pot
Comb decay pot
Compression pot
Filter width pot
Oscillator level pot
Pan pot
Pitch pot
Volume fader
Solo and Mute buttons
Sound name
