Pan subpane

The pan number-adjuster specifies the home pan position. This can also be controlled on the Mixer.

The envelope graph controls how the sounds pan changes throughout its duration. Left click to add or move points, right click to delete them.

The envelope range adjuster is the extent to which the pan envelope causes deviation from the home position.

Left/right click to decrease/increase number adjusters. Hold [ctrl] at the same time to fast-adjust them.
