What's new in v2?

NEW FEATURE:

Standalone version of AnarchyRhythms!

Instead of running through a vst host, this streams sound straight from your soundcard, through AnarchyRhythms, and back out your soundcard! Its parameters can be controlled with MIDI. It is shipped together with the vst version.

NEW FEATURE:

Multiple Patterns, with automatable switching between patterns! Specify 32 patterns within each patch, to program evolving rhythms!

NEW FEATURE:

Analog Shuffle. Adds a swing to AnarchyRhythms patterns! You can set any extent of shuffle from straight time to highly swung.

NEW FEATURE:

Comb Filter on each sound channel. This is set to the frequency of the pitch control and envelope, and typically brings out tuned humming tones with sonic qualities related to the input.
