Mixer Controls

Comb Decay

Specifies a feedback ratio for the comb filter. Comb filters run at the frequency of the pitch control and envelope.

Turning this value up will typically bring out tuned humming tones with sonic qualities related to your input.

Adjust mixer pots by left clicking on them and dragging the mouse up (clockwise) or down (anticlockwise).

This value can also be controlled on the Edit Pane.
