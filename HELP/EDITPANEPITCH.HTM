Pitch subpane

The coarse and fine tune pitch-adjusters control the home pitch of the channel's bandpass filter, comb filter, and internal oscillators. This can also be controlled on the Mixer.

The envelope graph controls how the sounds pitch changes throughout its duration. Left click to add or move points, right click to delete them.

The envelope range adjuster is the extent to which the pitch envelope causes deviation from the sounds home pitch.

Left/right click to decrease/increase adjusters. Hold [ctrl] at the same time to fast-adjust them.
