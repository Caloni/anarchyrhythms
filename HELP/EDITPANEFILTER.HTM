Filter width subpane

Filter width is the scope of the sounds bandpass filter above and below its pitch. Increasing filter width fattens the sound, decreasing it thins the sound.

The number-adjuster controls the overall filter width. Left/right click to decrease/increase it. Hold [ctrl] at the same time to fast-adjust.

The envelope graph controls how the sounds filter width changes throughout its duration. Left click to add or move points, right click to delete them.

Overall filter width can also be controlled on the Mixer.
