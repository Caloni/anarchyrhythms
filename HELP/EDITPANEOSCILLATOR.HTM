Oscillator subpane

Controls the internal oscillation to add to the sound. Using the internal oscillators pulls the sound away from the input, towards the purely synthetic domain.

The osc level adjuster specifies the overall level of the sound's internal oscillators. Left/right click to decrease/increase it, hold [ctrl] at the same time to fast-adjust. Oscillator level can also be controlled on the Mixer.

The waveform menus control the waveforms used for each sound's two oscillators. Click on the waveform names to choose between sine/saw/square/noise/external .wav file.

The envelope graph controls an interpolation path between the two oscillators. Left click to add or move points, right click to delete them.

The invert button swaps the phase of the second oscillator, causing nice cancellation effects when interpolating between sine/saw/square waves.
