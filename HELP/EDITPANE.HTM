Edit Pane

This provides nitty-gritty control over each channel. Only the currently highlighted channel is displayed, you can switch the highlight between channels by clicking on the channel tabstop at the top of the edit pane, or by clicking in the pattern editor or the mixer. Here's what the edit pane controls do:

Channel Tabstop

Volume subpane

Filter width subpane

Pan subpane

Pitch subpane

Oscillator subpane

Length adjuster

Feedback adjuster

Comb adjuster

Compression adjuster
